FAQ

how to cancel booking?

To cancel your booking, simply contact our customer support team via phone, email, or through our website. Please provide your booking reference and relevant details. Our team will assist you in processing the cancellation according to our policy. For any questions or assistance, feel free to reach out to us anytime.

when will I get the possession?

The possession date of your property is typically determined once all payment formalities are completed and legal processes are cleared. We will provide you with a clear timeline during the transaction process. For more specific details, feel free to contact our team, and we’ll be happy to assist you.

where can I pay the rent?

Rent payments can be made through multiple convenient methods, including online payment via our website, bank transfer, or in-person at our office. For detailed instructions or to set up automatic payments, please contact our support team or check your tenant portal for more options.

how to contact with the buyers?

To contact potential buyers, you can reach out through the contact details provided on property listings or through our platform. For direct communication, we also facilitate messaging via email, phone, or the inquiry form on our website. If you need assistance, our team can help connect you with interested buyers and ensure smooth communication throughout the process.

why my listing not showing up?

If your listing isn't showing up, it could be due to a few reasons:

1. Incomplete Information - Ensure all required details and photos are uploaded.
2. Approval Pending - Your listing might be awaiting approval or review.
3. Technical Issues - There may be a temporary glitch with the website.
4. Expired Listing - Double-check the listing date to ensure it hasn't expired. If you're still facing issues, please contact our support team, and we'll help resolve it promptly.

how to promote my listing?

To effectively promote your listing, consider these steps:

1. Enhance Your Listing - Add high-quality photos, detailed descriptions, and key features to make your property stand out.
2. Featured Listing - Upgrade your listing to a featured or premium option for better visibility.
3. Social Media Sharing - Share your listing on social media platforms to reach a broader audience.
4. Price Competitively - Ensure your pricing aligns with current market trends for higher interest.
If you need help, our team can assist in maximizing your listing’s exposure.
